P22 font Euro symbols key chart

P22 fonts from the last two years include a Euro symbol in the alt-0164 position. Some earlier fonts contain the symbol in other positions or not at all. this font presents the most complete selection of P22 Euro symbols available. They can be used with ther counterpart fonts when the need arises.
